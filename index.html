Welcome to the Official Map of Cardistry-Con! Here you will find a list of all the cardists attending this year's conference, as well as the countries they are from.

To navigate the map, zoom in or out accordingly, and the list will display all the cardists in that region. Clicking on a cardist's name will show you all the people from that same country, as will clicking on any of the pins on the map.

(NOTE: Pinned locations are not accurate to the city of each cardists' origin; they are just placed to give a rough sense of the general region the cardists are from.)
